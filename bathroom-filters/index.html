Bathroom Filters for Rightmove

Enhance your property search on Rightmove with easy-to-use bathroom filters. Quickly find homes that meet your criteria without the hassle.

Features

  • Add bathroom filters to your Rightmove property search.
  • Filter by minimum and maximum number of bathrooms.
  • Simple and intuitive interface.

Installation

  1. Go to the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Search for "Bathroom Filters for Rightmove".
  3. Click "Add to Chrome" and follow the prompts to install the extension.
